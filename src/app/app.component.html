About me
I am Anna, I am 27 years old, and I am a writer – out of passion. Since I was a teenager, I have been reading and writing a lot. The power of words is astonishing. One sentence can chance the surroundings of the reader from a sunny day to a cold, starless winter’s night. One paragraph can make a reader cry. One page can change their life. That is what always fascinated me about writing, about words. But when I grew older, another interest took shape: natural sciences. Currently, I am finishing my master’s degree in Geosciences. During the last years, I was able to learn about our planet and how it works, and about life itself. That is why I also love writing about natural sciences.
Services

Creative Writing

Fancy a story that was written just for yourself? The occasion or reason does not matter. It can be a present toyour husband or an e-book you want to sell on Amazon. What do you need to do to get it started? Naturally, this depends on what your requirements are. You can just give me a word and I’ll create a new world for you, or you can give me exact instructions on everything, and I’ll put it on paper. There are no limits – I am a creative thinker, so I do not need to follow the rules. It does not have to be a story that starts at A and ends at Z. What about a narrative that has a different ending every time you read it?
